The Idea

The world is complicated. Getting delicious pizza should be simple. It should also be private and secure. That's why we'll never ask you for your name or your payment details. Intrigued? Of course you are, here's how it works:

Tasty

We believe in delicious affordable pizza for the masses. One nation under Pie, indivisible. Liberty, pepperoni, for all.

Discreet

Data isn't always a good thing. We implement a Discreetza Pizzanonymous system. No cards. No addresses. No Names.

APM

Visit one of our Automated Pizza Machines to retrieve your pizza. All you need is your unique order id and some cash.

Go Incognit Dough

It's pretty simple. Choose a size, choose your toppings. Place your order. We'll give you a UUID to use at one of our APM's (automated pizza machines). All pizzas are ready in 15 minutes. Always. Insert your cash - take your pie. That's it. We'll use your internet session to bring up past orders. Turn off this feature anytime. Your privacy matters. So go on. Have a snaccident, with Sudo Pizza.
